>> Instruction on Oil Change

Tools & Materials Needed

  1. New motor oil (check viscosity)
  2. New oil filter (if replacing)
  3. Wrenches (socket to fit the drain plug)
  4. Oil drain pan and Funnel
  5. Shop rags or paper towels
  6. Gloves (disposable or mechanic's)
  7. New crush washer/gasket for the drain plug

Stepwise Instructions:

  • STEP 1: Warm up the Engine
  • STEP 2: Park in a Flat Stable Surface and Prepare
  • STEP 3: Place the Oil Drain Pan below the drain plug
  • STEP 4: Remove the drain plug using the correct Wrench Size, Be prepared for oil to start flowing immediately
  • STEP 5: Let the oil drain, May take several minutes
  • STEP 6 (optional): Replace the Oil Filter
  • STEP 7:Reinstall drain plug
  • STEP 8: Add new oil
  • STEP 9:Check oil level
  • STEP 10:Clean up
